Understanding Glaucoma

Facts about glaucoma

Glaucoma is a series of eye diseases that can cause damage to the optic nerve, resulting in visual field defects and vision loss. The most common form is corner open glaucoma, followed by corner closure glaucoma.

Recognizing Glaucoma

  • Severe eye pain
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Blurred vision
  • Halos around lights
